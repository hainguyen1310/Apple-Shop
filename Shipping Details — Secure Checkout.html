    • By entering a zip code, you’ll get estimated delivery dates for your items. You’ll get a final delivery date after you place your order. Delivery estimates are based on item availability and the shipping option you choose. Learn more about Apple Shipping (opens in a new window)

    • The shipping carrier decides whether an order requires a signature upon delivery. You can contact your carrier for more details. Learn more about Apple Shipping (opens in a new window)

    • Standard shipping is free for all online orders. Or you can upgrade your shipping method for an additional charge.

    • Shipping charges are calculated based on the size and weight of your entire order.

    • Each time an item ships from our warehouse, we’ll send you an email that includes the shipping carrier’s name and your order’s tracking number. You can click the link in the email to track the status of your package on the carrier’s website.

    • Apple offers several options for you to receive your order. You can presign for the package, sign with the shipping carrier to release your shipment to your delivery address, or pick up the package at the carrier’s local office. Learn more about Apple Shipping & Pickup (opens in a new window)

    • Apple can ship to APO/FPO addresses using a third-party freight forwarding service, which will redirect Apple Store shipments to APO/FPO addresses for a fee. Learn more about APO/FPO shipping (opens in a new window)

    • We’ll send you text messages when your items have shipped or when they’re ready for pickup. We may also contact you if there’s an issue with your order. Texts are sent between 8:00 a.m. and 9:00 p.m. in your time zone.

      To stop receiving text notifications, simply reply to the text message with "STOP."

    • Carrier-connected iPhone SE, iPhone 13, iPhone 14, iPhone 15, and iPhone 15 Pro models will arrive ready to activate with eSIM and can connect to your cellular voice and data service without a physical SIM card. If you completed the steps to authorize activation with AT&T, T-Mobile, or Verizon when you purchased your new iPhone online, it will arrive ready to use. Just turn it on and follow the onscreen instructions to set it up and activate with the carrier. To activate with eSIM, you will need Wi-Fi for setup. If you choose "Connect to a carrier later" when you buy your iPhone, you can activate with your service when you receive your device. If you're asked to transfer your SIM, follow the onscreen instructions to transfer service from your previous iPhone. To learn more, visit https://support.apple.com/kb/HT212780 (Opens in a new window).
